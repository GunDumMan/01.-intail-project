Welsh Corgi
MENU

The Welsh Corgi or Corgi, plural Corgis, or occasionally the etymologically consistent Corgwn is a small type of herding dog that originated in Wales. The name "corgi" is derived from the Welsh words "cor" and "ci" , meaning "dwarf" and "dog", respectively.
